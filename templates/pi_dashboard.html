Blinded Data Access

As a Principal Investigator, you have access only to blinded data:

  • Randomized codes (not patient identifiers)
  • OPG images
  • Sex information

You will not see:

  • Patient names or IDs
  • Actual ages
  • Which method (AlQahtani or Demirjian) is being used for each case

This blinding ensures unbiased age estimations and scientific rigor.
